Quantum Lens (Q-Lens):

Description: The Quantum Lens is a breakthrough wearable device that allows users to overlay augmented reality onto their vision in real-time. Using quantum processing, it adjusts the visual layer based on surroundings and user context, such as navigation assistance, real-time translations, and enhanced reality experiences for gaming and learning.

Highlights:

  • Augmented reality without the need for a screen.
  • First device ti use quantum technology for consumer-grade AR
  • Battery last for 72 hours of continuous use
